Welcome to Bosnia & Herzegovina.

Bosnia and Herzegovina, often referred to simply as Bosnia, is a country located in Southeast Europe on the Balkan Peninsula. It is known for its rich history, diverse cultural heritage, and stunning natural landscapes. The country is bordered by Croatia to the north and west, Serbia to the east, and Montenegro to the southeast. Sarajevo, the capital city, is famous for its blend of Ottoman, Austro-Hungarian, and modern influences, as well as its vibrant cultural scene.
